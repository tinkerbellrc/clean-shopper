Clean Shopper

Visual Design System

A knowledgeable friend who's already done the research — warm, unhurried, and trustworthy. Every visual choice reinforces two things: this product is grounded in nature, and it gives you exactly what you need to make a confident decision, nothing more.

05 — AI Conversation
Conversational Interface

No chat bubbles. AI responses appear as clean body text. Feels like reading, not messaging. User input sits in a linen bar at the bottom.

06 — AI States
How the UI Communicates AI Activity

Six states to design for. Visual signals should be informative, never alarming.

Thinking
Three sage dots, soft pulse. No spinner. Appears below last message, not full-screen.
Suggesting
Cards fade in with 100ms stagger. Feels deliberate, not instant-dump.
Confident
Best match
Moss badge on the leading recommendation. Used only when the AI is certain.
Uncertain
Here are a few options worth considering.
No badge. Copy shifts to open-ended framing. No visual alarm — just honest.
Preference Applied
Based on your fragrance-free preference
Stone label with sage left border. Quiet acknowledgment the system is paying attention.
Flagged Ingredient
Synthetic fragrance
